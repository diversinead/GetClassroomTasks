## Quick formula reference

| Indicator | Formula |
|---|---|
| Net Profit Margin (NPM) | Net Profit ÷ Sales × 100 |
| Return on Investment (ROI) | Net Profit ÷ Avg. Owner's Investment × 100 |
| Working Capital Ratio (WCR) | Current Assets ÷ Current Liabilities |
| GST balance | Opening + CRJ GST + SJ GST − CPJ GST − PJ GST |
| Profit / Loss | Revenues − Expenses |
